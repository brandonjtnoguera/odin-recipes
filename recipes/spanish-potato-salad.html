Spanish potato salad

This is a common Spanish dish, very important in Spanish culinary history. The dish is made up of a variety of vegetables and spices.

Ingredients

>

  • 1 Red potato
  • 3 Tablespoons of Olvie oil
  • 1 Tablespoon of red wine vinegar
  • 2 Crushed Garlic cloves
  • 1 Red bell pepper
  • 2 Pimento-stuffed green olives
  • 1 Tablespoon of minced shallots
  • 1 Tablespoon of Chopped parsely
  • Salt and ground black pepper
  • 1 Lemon cut into wedges

Steps

  1. Place potatoes into a pot and cover with salted water; bring to a boil.Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until tender, about 10 minutes. Drain and cool u ntil easily handled. Slice potatoes about 1/2-inch thick and place in a bowl.
  2. Whisk olive oil and vinegar together in a bowl; stir in garlic. Pour dressing over potatoes and lightly toss to coat.
  3. Mix red bell pepper, green olives, shallot, parsley, salt, and pepper into potatoes. Serve salad with lemon wedges to squeeze over each serving.
